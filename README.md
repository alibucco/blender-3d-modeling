# Orient Express Reimagined – 3D Environment (Blender)

## Overview
3D environment design project inspired by a reinterpretation of the Orient Express train interior.
The scene depicts a train wagon divided into two functional spaces by a central bar counter: a bar area and a restaurant area, both designed within the same architectural volume.
The environment is set during early morning light to enhance atmosphere, mood and material perception.

---

## Concept
The goal of the project was to explore spatial storytelling through interior design, combining architecture, lighting and composition.
The environment merges two social functions within a single coherent space, emphasizing contrast and interaction between them.

---

## Key Focus
- Interior environment design  
- Spatial composition and layout  
- Lighting design (morning atmosphere)  
- Material and mood exploration
